Fluffy Pancakes

How to make delicious pancakes

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp white vinegar
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp white table sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp butter(melted)
  • cooking spray

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl combine milk and vinegar and set aside for 5 minutes.
  2. Combine all dry ingredients.
  3. Whisk egg and butter into the milk and vinegar mixture.
  4. Slowly mix dry into the wet ingredients. Mix until lumps are gone.
  5. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and pour 1/4 cup of the mixture into the pan covered that has been sprayed with cooking spray. Flip with a spatula once bubbles appear.
